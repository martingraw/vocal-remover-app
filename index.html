How It Works

Vocal Remover Pro uses advanced AI technology to separate vocals from instrumental tracks in your music files. Here's how it works:

1

Upload Your Audio

Select an audio file (MP3, WAV, etc.) by dragging and dropping it into the upload area or clicking to browse your files.

2

AI Processing

Our application uses Spleeter, a powerful AI-based source separation library, to analyze your audio and separate it into different components:

  • 2-stems model: Separates into vocals and accompaniment
  • 4-stems model: Separates into vocals, drums, bass, and other
  • 5-stems model: Separates into vocals, drums, bass, piano, and other

You can select your preferred model in the Settings.

3

Results

Based on your selection in Settings, Spleeter separates the audio into 2, 4, or 5 distinct stems. You will receive each of these separated stems as individual audio files:

  • 2-stems model: You'll get 'vocals' and 'accompaniment'.
  • 4-stems model: You'll get 'vocals', 'drums', 'bass', and 'other'.
  • 5-stems model: You'll get 'vocals', 'drums', 'bass', 'piano', and 'other'.

You can play these directly in your browser or download them for use in your projects.

Note: The quality of separation depends on the complexity of the original recording and the model used. For best results, use high-quality audio files and experiment with different models.
